Health

Spay vs Neuter


Spay & Neuter helps control the animal population. It can also help reduce the chances of certain diseases like cancer. This is a procedure that is done at 4 to 6 months of age depending on the individual kitten. This can be done at any age if you have an adult cat.


Spay
- For female kittens or cats.
- Removal of a female's uterus and ovaries.
- More complicated procedure than neutering.
Neuter
- For male kittens or cats.
- Removal of a male's reproductive organs.
- Simple and painless process.



Health Care


Annual checkups are very important for cats. The vet would do a full body checkup such as checking for any possible heart murmurs, teeth checkup, poop sample delivery etc. During these checkups, cats that are due for vaccines are given them.


Annual Checkup
- Feels parts of the body for any abnormalities
- Listens to the heart with a stethoscope for any heart murmurs
- Weight checking
- Checks genital area
- Takes poop samples to test for any abnormalities

Vaccinations
Core Vaccines (highly recommended)
- Rabies
- FVRCP
Noncore Vaccines (sometimes recommended)
- Bordetella
- Chalmydia
